Research interests

  1. Geochronological method development
    • Geochronological data reduction and plotting with IsoplotR
      inverse isochrons and errorchrons - high MSWDs
    • U-Pb geochronology: total-Pb/U–Th isochrons - MDA estimation
      QEMSCAN + LAICPMS - redefining discordance - SIMS data processing - Taung
    • Fission tracks: LAICPMS based dating - book chapter
    • 40Ar/39Ar: data reduction and error propagation
    • U-Th-He: overdispersion - averaging - mineral inclusions - in-situ dating using proton-induced 3He.
    • Thermal history modelling: HeFTy vs. QTQt
  2. Sedimentary provenance analysis
    • The visualisation and intercomparison of dissimilar detrital age distributions
    • Point-counting data, varietal and multi-method provenance data
    • A tutorial in statistical provenance analysis using the provenance package
    • How many grains is enough? - calculators
    • Detrital thermochronology of the Great Valley Group, the White Mountains and the Nubian Sandstone
  3. Cosmogenic nuclide dating
    • Cosmogenic nuclide dating and neutrino physics
    • Interlaboratory comparison of 21Ne in quartz
    • Cosmogenic 3He and 21Ne in artificial quartz targets
    • Cosmogenic nuclide data reduction
  4. Aeolian geomorphology
    • Monitoring dune migration rates as a windiness proxy in the central Sahara
    • Solitary wave behaviour in sand dunes - animations
    • The provenance and dynamics of the Namib and Taklamakan deserts
    • Measuring sand flux by remote sensing
  5. Geochemistry
    • Tectonic discrimination with discriminant analysis and decision trees
    • Logratio analysis of the tholeiitic and calc-alkaline magma suites
