Muscovite KAl3Si2O10(OH,F)2

Thin-Section.

Colour. Colourless to pale green

Pleochroism. Usually colourless.

Habit. Platy

Cleavage. Perfect in 1 direction

Relief. Low

Birefringence. Strong to 2nd order greens and blues

Twinning. Very rare.

Extinction. Parallel

Distinguishing features. Colour, cleavage and birefringence
