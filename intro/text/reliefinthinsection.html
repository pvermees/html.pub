Relief

Look at this garnet in PPL. Notice how it appears to stand out against the surrounding minerals.

This is known as relief and bcause it 'stands out' garnet is said to have high relief.

Relief is caused by light rays travelling slower through a mineral compared with the surrounding minerals producing an illusion of thickness.

The minerals surrounding the garnet have low relief and biotite (the brown mineral in PPL) is said to have moderate relief.
