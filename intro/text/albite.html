Sodic-Feldspar (Albite) NaAlSi3O8

Thin-Section.

Colour. Colourless

Pleochroism. None

Habit. Anhedral to subhedral 'laths'

Cleavage. 1 perfect, 1 good, 2 imperfect

Relief. Low

Birefringence. Low to 1st order grey

Twinning. Common (Albite twinning)

Extinction. Inclined. Determine plagioclase type by Michel Levy angle

Distinguishing features. Low relief, Michel Levy angle and twinning
