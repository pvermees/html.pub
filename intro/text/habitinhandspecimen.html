Habit or crystal form

Mineral growth is governed by its crystal structure. This is called its Habit which can be sub-divided into 9 basic groups
  1. Acicular or needle-like - andalusite
  2. Bladed - kyanite
  3. Botryoidal - hematite
  4. Fibrous - sillimanite
  5. Massive
  6. Tabular
  7. Platy
  8. Prismatic
