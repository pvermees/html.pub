Garnet.

Thin-Section.

Colour. Colourless to light pink, red or brown.

Pleocroism. Weak.

Habit. Prismatic commonly euhedral, dodecahedral or trapezohedral. Inclusions common.

Relief. Very high

Cleavage. None but fractures common.

Birefringence. Isotropic which means they are in extinction permanently

Twinning. Isotropic

Extinction. Isotropic
