Sillimanite Al2SiO5

Thin-Section.

Colour. Colourless to light yellow

Pleochroism. None

Habit. Fibrous or acicular

Cleavage. 1 good cleavage normal (90o) to length of crystal

Relief. Fairly high

Birefringence. Moderate to 2nd order blues and purples

Twinning. None

Extinction. Parallel

Distinguishing features. Fibrous and/or acicular habit. Distinctive transverse fracture.
