Andalusite Al2SiO5

Thin-Section.

Colour. Colourless

Pleochroism. None

Habit. Acicular but transverse sections are square. Dark inclusions of carbonaceous material is frequently arranged symmetrically to form a Maltese Cross. This variety of Andalusite is called Chiastolite.

Cleavage. 2 moderately good cleavages in cross-section

Relief. Fairly high

Birefringence. Weak up to 1st order yellows

Twinning.

Extinction. Parallel
