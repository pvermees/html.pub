Quartz - SiO2

Thin-section

Colour Colourless.

Pleochroism No pleochroism.

Cleavage No cleavage but often shows irregular fracture.

Habit Generally anhedral.

Relief Very low (positive).

Birefringence Weak 1st order greys.

Twinning Not visible in thin-section.

Extinction Unstrained quartz shows UNIT extinction where it goes dark in XPL four times in a 360o rotation. Quartz also commonly shows undulose extinction which indicates that the quartz lattice has been strained.

Distinguishing features Colourless, low relief and undulose extinction.
