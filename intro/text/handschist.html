This hand specimen clearly shows compositional variation, i.e. dark layers and lighter layers alternating throughout the rock. The dark bands are composed of mafic minerals such as biotite and garnet and the lighter bands are mainly made of quartz and feldspar. When writing your opening statement, you must consider the grain size, the overall colour and the larger porphyroblasts of garnet up to 2mm in size and of course the compositional layering.
