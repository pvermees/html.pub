Amphibole (Hornblende)

Thin-Section.

Colour. Green or brown

Pleochroism. Strong shades of green

Habit. Euhedral to subhedral

Cleavage. 2 good cleavages at ~120o

Relief. High

Birefringence. Moderate to 2nd order reds

Twinning. Occasional, but can be common in volcanic rocks.

Extinction. Inclined

Distinguishing features. Colour, pleochroism and cleavage
