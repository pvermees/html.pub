Birefringence

Mineralogist and petrologists use both plane-polarised light (PPL) and cross-polarised light (XPL) to identify the mineral they are looking at. You need to use the analyser on your microscope to do this.

,/p> When the analyser is inserted you will immediately notice the multiple colours in the thin-section. In XPL these colours are known as the birefringence colours, often shortened to birefringence.
