Mineral B

This is all the same mineral but it has a different form in this image and is more fibrous.

Watch carefully the different birefringence colours as you rotate this mineral in XPL

You will note it is much more variable.

This mineral is sillimanite and normally you see it in acicular form. However, you do find this more fibrous form which is known as fibrolite.
