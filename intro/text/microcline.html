Potassic or Alkaline-Feldspar KAlSi3O8 (Microcline)

Thin-Section.

Colour. Colourless

Pleochroism. None

Habit. Subhedral to Anhedral

Cleavage. 1 perfect, 1 good, 2 imperfect

Relief. Low

Birefringence. Weak to 1st order grey

Twinning. 'Tartan' polysynthetic very common.

Extinction. Oblique

Distinguishing features. Low relief and 'tartan' twinning
