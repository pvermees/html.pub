Kyanite Al2SiO5

Thin-Section.

Colour. Colourless to pale blue

Pleochroism. Occasional in larger sections

Habit. Tabular

Cleavage. 2 good cleavages

Relief. High

Birefringence. Moderate to 1st order reds

Twinning. Frequent

Extinction. Oblique

Distinguishing features. Colour, extinction angle, bladed nature of crystal.
