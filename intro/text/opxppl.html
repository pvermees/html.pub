Orthopyroxene (Mg, Fe) SiO3 (Enstatite)

The orthopyroxene group consists of enstatite and hypersthene. The main diagnostic property is the parallel extinction

Thin-Section.

Colour.

Pleochroism. Very weak-slight pink to green

Habit. Usually subhedral prismatic. Square in cross-section

Cleavage. 2 good cleavages at 90o

Relief. High

Birefringence. Weak to 1st order yellow/orange

Twinning. Rare

Extinction. Parallel
