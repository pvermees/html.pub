Clinopyroxene Ca(Mg,Fe)Si2O6

Clinopyroxenes are chemically varied and comprise diopside, augite, pigeonite,hedenbergite,aegerine-augite,aegerine and jadeite but the most common types are augite and diopside.

Thin-Section.

Colour.

Pleochroism. None

Habit. Usually subhedral prismatic

Cleavage. 2 good cleavages at 87o to 93o

Relief. High

Birefringence. Strong to 2nd order greens and reds

Twinning. Common

Extinction. Oblique (45o)

Distinguishing features. 2 good cleavages at ~90o, high birefringence, twinning and oblique extinction.
