Olivine. (Mg, Fe)2SiO4

Thin-Section.

Colour. Colourless

Pleochroism. None

Habit. Euhedral olivine can be rectangular or six-sidedl

Cleavage. One very poor. Commonly shows irregular fractures that are filled with a highly birefringent mineral Serpentine

Relief. Fairly high.

Birefringence. Strong up to 2nd order reds.

Twinning. Rarely seen but can be well developed, especially in volcanic rocks. Can also be zoned.

Extinction. Parallel to crystal edges.

Distinguishing features. High birefringence, high relief and irregular fractures
